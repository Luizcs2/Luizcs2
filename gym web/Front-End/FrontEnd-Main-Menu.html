Welcome to Motiv8! We are the #1 fitness app based in Dublin, Ireland. By browsing our services, you get access to a vast amount of workout videos, workout plans, nutrition plans, and more. Motiv8 is a sleek and user-friendly fitness app developed by Alicia, Darren, Francisco, Luiz, Patrick, and Tom of Group 7 for our CS353 project. Motiv8 offers personalized workouts, an extensive exercise library, and user profiles to track your fitness journey. Connect with fellow fitness enthusiasts, share achievements, and stay motivated with challenges. Whether you're a fitness pro or just starting, Motiv8 is your go-to companion for a healthier lifestyle.
